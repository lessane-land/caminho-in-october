An invitation · to ourselves

For us, Gala mine,

Let's go to the Costa Brava.

I've been thinking. The Mediterranean is still warm in October. The light turns gold. The tourists go home. The fishermen still bring anchovies in at sunrise. And we, amor, deserve a week that belongs to no one but us.

So I imagined us a week. October 1st to 8th, 2026. Seven days. One little fishing town called L'Escala. A car. A coast carved with hidden coves. Dalí's strange beautiful houses, because of course, my Gala. The Camí de Ronda along cliffs that fall straight into turquoise water.

You. Me. The sea. Let's book the tickets.
Let's say yes to us, and go.
(Catalan for "yes, let's go")

03 · Els dies

Day by day.

Eight days. The middle ones are anchored in L'Escala, we wander out and come back.

Day 01
Thu · Oct 1

Madrid → Girona → L'Escala

AVE to Girona. Pick up the car at the station. 40 minutes north and we're at the apartment. Sunset walk along the port, dinner somewhere with anchovies on the menu.

Train Car Dinner
Day 02
Fri · Oct 2

L'Escala + Empúries on foot

Coffee in town, then the coastal walk to Empúries. Roman and Greek ruins right on the sea, one of the most beautiful archaeological sites in the Mediterranean. We can swim after.

Culture Walking Beach
Day 03
Sat · Oct 3

Cadaqués + Portlligat

50 min drive along the curves. Salvador Dalí's house in Portlligat, the strangest and most personal of his spaces. Limited tickets, very limited capacity. Lunch in Cadaqués, dinner at Casa Anita (no reservations, just show up early).

Dalí Coves Book ahead
Dalí House tickets Casa Anita on map
Day 04
Sun · Oct 4

Figueres · Dalí Theatre-Museum

35 minutes to Figueres. The Dalí Theatre-Museum is the most surreal museum in Spain. Built by him, designed by him, he's buried in it. Lunch at El Motel or Hotel Empordà for proper Catalan country cooking.

between us

Dalí built the Theatre-Museum in Figueres as a love letter to Gala. He's buried there with her. And we both put it on Day 4. We didn't plan that. But also, of course we did.

Museum Food
Theatre-Museum tickets
Day 05
Mon · Oct 5

Camí de Ronda · Roses → Cap de Creus

The trail along the cliffs. We've walked Caminos before, this one's different. Turquoise water below, salt-shrubs on either side, fishing boats coming back in. Start at Roses, see how far our legs take us toward Cadaqués.

Trekking GR-92
Day 06
Tue · Oct 6

Cap de Creus Natural Park

A landscape that doesn't look like anywhere else in Europe. Wind-carved rocks, almost lunar. We drive to the lighthouse at the very tip, stay for sunset, dinner at the old lighthouse restaurant.

Nature Sunset
Day 07
Wed · Oct 7
our last full day

L'Escala slow · anchovies & nothing

No car today. Market in the morning, anchovy tasting at the source. Anchoas Solés has been salting fish here since 1898. A long lunch. A swim if the weather plays along. Farewell dinner wherever the day takes us.

Anchovies Slow
Day 08
Thu · Oct 8

Back to Madrid

Check-out, drop the car at Girona, train south. Goodbye Costa Brava. Fins aviat.

Return
